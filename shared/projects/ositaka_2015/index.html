About Ositaka

Nuno Marques is the "master-mind" from behind the mask titled "ositaka". Born in the late 80's has always been influenced and inspired by the pixels of video-games, electrifying colors of the season and supersonic mathematical melodies - which unfolded at the time, between 4 and 8-bits.

After obtaining two courses in Graphic Design and Multimedia, here comes the time to venture out on German lands - Munich - at 19 years of age, where he worked for two European agencies of astronomy (HUBBLE/ESA & ESO).

Today days, and after working in a half-dozen creative agencies, develops work as freelance Web Developer and Designer for TwoImpulse, leading company based in Zurich.
